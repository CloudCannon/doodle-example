# Welcome

To contribute a doodle:

* Click this link to open [The Doodler](cloudcannon:/collections/pages:/edit?collection=pages&amp;path=%2Fsrc%2Fpages%2Findex.astro)
* Draw something on the canvas, then click the Submit button on the page.
* This will create a couple of changes. Click the big save button in the top-right corner to commit them to the site. A couple minutes later, your artwork will appear on the homepage!
